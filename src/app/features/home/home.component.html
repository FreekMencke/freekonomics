Automatically Sync Accounts

Thanks to the PSD2 legislation in europe, the balance of your current accounts can be updated automatically once they have been linked.

Some banks also support linking savings accounts using PSD2. Otherwise you can create custom accounts which can be updated manually.

Track Your Recent Transactions

You can look up your transactions up to 3 months in the past.

A lot of data involved with transactions is dependant on how well banks have implemented the PSD2 API. This means that the quality in data between banks may differ.

Your Data Is Private and Secure

Freekonomics does not store any of your data remotely. All data is saved locally on your device, ensuring that your information remains private and secure.

For the requisitions of your bank accounts, we use GoCardless (previously Nordigen). Please read their privacy policy for more information.

We use Google Analytics to anonymously track usage data, which helps us understand how users interact with our service and which features are most popular.
